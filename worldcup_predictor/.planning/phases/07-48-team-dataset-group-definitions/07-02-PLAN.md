    - Each group has exactly 6 matches in `"matches"`

    - No team appears in more than one group
    - All 6 match_ids per group follow pattern `GS_{group}_01` through `GS_{group}_06`
    - All match_ids are unique across all 12 groups (72 unique IDs)
