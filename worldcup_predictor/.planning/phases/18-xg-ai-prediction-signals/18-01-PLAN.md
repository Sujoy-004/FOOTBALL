<objective>
**xG Extraction & Lambda Override in Group Simulation**

**Purpose:** Extract `expected_home_goals` / `expected_away_goals` from the existing BSD predictions endpoint during `parse_catboost_response()`, then make these values available as optional lambda overrides in `precompute_matchup_lambdas()`. This gives more accurate Poisson scoreline distribution (and therefore better tiebreaker resolution) without introducing xG as a separate blender signal (per D-01).

**Output:** Modified `catboost.py` (xG extraction), `groups.py` (lambda override), `knockout.py` (param forwarding), `test_groups.py` (tests).
</objective>
